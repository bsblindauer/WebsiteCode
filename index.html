Meet Brian Blindauer

I'm Brian, a photographer based in the Palm Springs, CA area, with a passion for capturing the world's stillness and the rapid pace of development. My work is divided between fine art landscape photography and high-resolution commercial aerial documentation.

For landscape, I chase fleeting moments of light and atmosphere, aiming to produce prints that evoke a sense of calm and wonder. My technical focus involves long exposures and blending traditional photography with modern digital techniques.

In my commercial work, I specialize in providing precise, timely, and compliant drone documentation for construction sites, real estate, and project managers. I hold a Part 107 FAA certification and prioritize safe, reliable operations to deliver critical visual data.

Whether it's capturing a magnificent sunset or tracking the progress of a multi-million dollar build, my goal is always clarity, quality, and impactful storytelling.
